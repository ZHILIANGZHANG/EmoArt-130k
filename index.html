About EmoArt

EmoArt is a pioneering large-scale dataset designed for emotional analysis and generation in artistic images. Comprising over 132,000 artworks, it spans Western and Eastern art traditions, from Renaissance to contemporary movements, bridging the gap between visual art and emotion understanding. The dataset includes multi-dimensional annotations generated by GPT-4o with human validation (>85% agreement), covering emotional, visual, and therapeutic dimensions. EmoArt enables AI systems to create emotionally expressive artistic content, supporting applications in affective computing, art therapy, and creative design.

Annotation Schema

Content Description

Detailed, emotionally-aware descriptions averaging 35.6 words with rich narrative content and artistic context

Visual Attributes

Five artistic elements: Brushwork, Composition, Color, Line, and Light with structured analysis

Valence & Arousal

Binary emotional dimensions based on Russell's circumplex model for systematic emotion mapping

Emotion Categories

12 representative emotions spanning the full valence-arousal space for interpretable classification

Therapeutic Potential

Assessment of potential psychological benefits for art therapy and wellness applications
