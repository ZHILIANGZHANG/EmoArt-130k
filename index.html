About the Dataset

EmoArt is a pioneering large-scale dataset designed for emotional analysis and generation in artistic images. Comprising over 132,000 artworks with multi-dimensional annotations, it bridges the gap between visual art and emotion understanding.

Dataset Highlights

Comprehensive Collection

132,000+ artworks spanning Western and Eastern traditions, from Renaissance to contemporary movements

Rich Annotations

Multi-dimensional annotations generated by GPT-4o with human validation (>85% agreement)

Emotional Dimensions

12 representative emotions spanning the full valence-arousal space for interpretable classification

Visual Analysis

Five artistic elements: Brushwork, Composition, Color, Line, and Light with structured analysis

Train models to generate images with specific emotional expressions and artistic styles based on detailed prompts and annotations

Affective Computing
